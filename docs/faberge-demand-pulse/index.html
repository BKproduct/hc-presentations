Processing logic, eight steps

1
Parallel collection
Each source runs in a separate worker weekly with full provenance.
2
Normalisation and tagging
One schema + curated jewellery lexicon for cross-platform comparability.
3
Signal strength scoring
Combines volume, intent purity, recency, and source quality.
4
Theme aggregation
Top demand themes grouped by lexicon categories each week.
5
Surge / decline detection
Rolling baseline and z-scores classify rising vs fading signals.
6
Buyer cohort tagging
UHNW, HENRY, Asia UHNW, and Gen Z mapped on each theme.
7
Synthesis on Opus 4.7
Turns raw evidence into design, sales, and marketing decisions.
8
Render and deliver
Weekly PDF bulletin + executive summary + draft email.
